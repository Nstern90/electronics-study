# Electronics Engineering Curriculum

This document maps the curriculum followed in this repository. It serves as a guide to the sequence of topics and associated resources.

## Phase 1: Circuits and Electronics
- MIT 6.002 Circuits and Electronics
  https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-002-circuits-and-electronics-spring-2007/

- All About Circuits Textbook
  https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/textbook/

## Phase 2: Signals and Systems
- MIT 6.003 Signals and Systems
  https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-003-signals-and-systems-fall-2011/

## Phase 3: Digital Logic
- MIT 6.111 Introductory Digital Systems Laboratory
  https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-111-introductory-digital-systems-laboratory-fall-2002/

## Phase 4: Microcontrollers
- MIT 6.01SC Introduction to EECS I
  https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-01sc-introduction-to-electrical-engineering-and-computer-science-i-spring-2011/

## Phase 5: PCB Design
- KiCad Like a Pro (official course site)
  https://techexplorations.com/so/kicad-like-a-pro/

- KiCad Official Documentation
  https://docs.kicad.org/

## Phase 6: Electromagnetics
- MIT 6.013 Electromagnetics and Applications
  https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-013-electromagnetics-and-applications-spring-2009/

## Phase 7: Control Systems
- MIT 6.01SC Control Modules
  https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-01sc-introduction-to-electrical-engineering-and-computer-science-i-spring-2011/pages/unit-3-signals-and-systems/

- MIT 6.241J Dynamic Systems and Control
  https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-241j-dynamic-systems-and-control-spring-2011/
